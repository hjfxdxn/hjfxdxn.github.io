Race Report

Mt. Fuji Hill Climb Silver Ring

Time: 1:10:25 · Silver Ring · Men 19-24: 43/354 · Overall: 533/7,887

Three recon rides since April, none of them felt great. Zwift said sub-1:15 was possible, but the real mountain always tells the truth.

Started near the back of Wave 3 thanks to last year's Bronze Ring result. The field quickly split into small groups, so I rode my own pace, bridged gaps when opportunities came, and found a few random teammates to share turns with along the way.

With 15 km to go, I realized Silver was secure. Gold was out of reach, so I backed off slightly, fueled up, and saved something for the final flat section.

The upper slopes were covered in thick fog. Couldn't see the riders ahead, couldn't see the road, just kept pushing. The final sprint through the fog was absolutely insane.

Made it safely through the two dark, wet tunnels near the finish and gave everything I had to the line.

Silver Ring Men 19-24: 43/354 Age 21: 15/74 Overall: 533/7,887

From Bronze Ring last year to Silver Ring this year. Not bad for a day on Fuji.
